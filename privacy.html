中文

  • 数据存储：你创建的行程、景点、备注、团队人数等，全部保存在本机。如果你在系统里登录了 iCloud，App 会用你自己的 iCloud 私有存储在你的设备间同步——这些数据只属于你，开发者无法访问。
  • 位置信息：仅在你授权「使用 App 期间」时，用于实时计算到下一个景点的车程。位置不被存储、不被上传，只在当下用于算路。
  • 客人信息 AI 导入（可选，带团功能）：当你使用此功能时，你所选的图片 / 截图 / PDF / 文字会发送到云端 AI 服务（阿里云百炼 Qwen-VL，OpenAI 兼容接口）做文字识别，其中可能包含客人姓名 / 电话 / 地址等个人信息。识别结果只保存在你的设备上；App 不保存你上传的原图 / 原文。该处理受阿里云的隐私政策约束。你也可以不使用 AI、改为手动录入，届时不发生任何上传。导游有责任在收集 / 录入客人个人信息前取得客人同意。客人信息不进备份、不上传 iCloud。
  • 第三方地图服务（可选）：如果你自行在「设置」里填入了高德或 Google 的 API key，App 会用你的 key 直接向对应服务请求路况 / 营业时间。这类请求只发出「地点名称 + 经纬度坐标」（以及你自己的 key），不会发送你的行程安排、备注、团队人数等其它任何数据。受该服务自己的隐私政策约束。不填则不发生此类请求。
  • 导出的备份文件：你主动导出的备份是未加密的明文 JSON，包含你的行程、景点与备注（不含客人信息）。它只存在于你导出到的位置，请妥善保管。
  • 诊断：App 用苹果的 MetricKit 在本机记录崩溃 / 卡顿日志，不上传任何地方。
  • 无广告、无分析、无第三方追踪 SDK。

English

No ads, no analytics, no third-party tracking. Itinerary data stays on your own device; the only data that ever leaves your device is what you explicitly send when using the optional “AI client import” feature, plus map requests when you supply your own map key.

  • Storage: Itineraries, places, notes, and group counts are stored on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, the app syncs across your own devices via your private iCloud storage — only you can access it; the developer cannot.
  • Location: Used only with your “While Using the App” permission, to compute live drive time to your next stop. Location is never stored or uploaded; it is used only in the moment for routing.
  • AI client import (optional, guide feature): When you use this feature, the images / screenshots / PDFs / text you select are sent to a cloud AI service (Alibaba Cloud Bailian, Qwen-VL, via an OpenAI-compatible API) for text recognition. They may contain client names / phone numbers / addresses. The recognized result is stored only on your device; the app does not retain the originals you upload. This processing is subject to Alibaba Cloud’s privacy policy. You may skip AI and enter data manually instead, in which case nothing is uploaded. As a guide, you are responsible for obtaining your clients’ consent before collecting or entering their personal information. Client info is never included in backups or uploaded to iCloud.
  • Optional third-party map services: If you enter your own AutoNavi (Amap) or Google API key in Settings, the app uses your key to request traffic / opening hours directly from that service. These requests send only the place name + latitude/longitude (plus your own key); they never send your itinerary, notes, group counts, or any other data. Subject to that service’s privacy policy. With no key entered, no such requests are made.
  • Exported backup files: A backup you export is an unencrypted plain-text JSON containing your itineraries, places, and notes (not client info). It lives only where you export it — keep it safe.
  • Diagnostics: The app records crash/hang logs locally via Apple MetricKit. They are never uploaded.
  • No ads, no analytics, no third-party tracking SDKs.
